ℹ️ Your platform (Apple or Google) may require you to sign in again before allowing account deletion. This is a security measure to prevent accidental removals.

2Request deletion via email

If you cannot access the app — due to a lost device, forgotten credentials, or broken sign-in — you can request deletion by emailing us.

  • Send an email to info@thekada.in
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address registered to your DevFlow account
  • We may need to verify your identity before processing the request
⚠️ Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your workspace data — including projects, clients, invoices, and files — cannot be recovered.

3What gets deleted

When your account deletion is processed, the following data is scheduled for removal:

👤Account profile & credentials
📋All projects and tasks
👥Client records
🎯Leads and pipeline data
💳Quotes & invoice records
📁Uploaded files & documents
📊Analytics & usage data
⚙️Settings & branding preferences

4What may be retained

Some data may be retained after account deletion where required by law, to prevent fraud, or for operational necessity:

  • Transaction records required for tax compliance or financial reporting.
  • Minimal records needed to respond to legal requests, disputes, or enforcement actions.
  • Aggregated, anonymised analytics data that cannot be linked back to your identity.

Retained data is held only for as long as legally or operationally necessary, then permanently purged.

5Subscriptions & billing

Deleting your DevFlow account does not automatically cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately through your platform's subscription management:

  • iOS: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → DevFlow → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → DevFlow → Cancel

DevFlow does not process refunds directly. Refund requests should be submitted to Apple or Google through their standard process.

6Deletion timeline

  • Account access is removed immediately upon confirmation.
  • Workspace data (Firestore records, files) is scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
  • Backup systems may retain data for an additional 90 days before permanent purge.
  • Legal and compliance-required records are retained as described above.

7Need help?

If you have any questions about account deletion, data handling, or your rights, contact us:

✉️ info@thekada.in

Subject: "Account Deletion Request" · Include your registered email address · We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
