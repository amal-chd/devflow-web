Data Rights

Account Deletion

Clear instructions for deleting a DevFlow account and requesting related data deletion.

Delete your account in the app

The current app includes an in-app delete account flow. Inside the app, open:

  1. Profile
  2. Security & Privacy
  3. Delete Account

You may be asked to sign in again if your platform requires recent authentication before deletion.

What happens when you delete your account

  • Your DevFlow account access is removed.
  • Workspace data associated with that account is scheduled for deletion, subject to legal, fraud-prevention, and operational retention requirements.
  • Uploaded files, project data, and related records may no longer be recoverable once deletion is completed.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need help completing a deletion request, contact support@devflow.com from the email address associated with your account.
