Sign-in & accounts

Having trouble accessing your account? Here's what usually helps.

  • Check that you're using the correct sign-in method (email / Google / Apple)
  • Use your platform's account recovery if you've lost access to your email
  • Reset your password via the sign-in screen
Get sign-in help →
💳

Subscriptions & billing

All subscription management happens through the App Store or Google Play.

  • Go to your device's App Store / Play Store → Subscriptions to manage DevFlow
  • Cancellations and refunds follow Apple / Google policies, not ours
  • For plan upgrade questions, email us and we'll help
Contact for billing →
🗂️

Data & privacy requests

You have full rights to your DevFlow data at any time.

  • Request an export of your workspace data via email
  • Correction requests: email us with specific details
  • Deletion: use in-app flow or see our Account Deletion page
Read Privacy Policy →
📱

App issues & bugs

If you encounter a crash or unexpected behaviour, we want to know.

  • Include your device model, iOS/Android version, and app version
  • Attach a screenshot or screen recording if possible
  • Describe the steps that led to the issue
Report a bug →
🧹

Account deletion

Want to remove your DevFlow account and all associated data?

  • In-app: Profile → Security & Privacy → Delete Account
  • Via web: see our dedicated Account Deletion page
  • Can't access the app? Email us from your registered account address
Deletion guide →

Response expectations

What to include for faster triage.

  • Account email address tied to your DevFlow profile
  • Device and OS version (e.g. iPhone 15 / iOS 18)
  • Screenshots or screen recording of the issue
  • Clear description of what happened vs. what you expected

Typical response: 1–2 business days
