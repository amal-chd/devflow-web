Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This policy explains how DevFlow handles personal data when people use the mobile app, support channels, and related services.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the DevFlow mobile application, public website pages, customer support interactions, and related services operated under the DevFlow name.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use DevFlow, we may collect:

  • Account information such as name, email address, sign-in method, and profile details.
  • Business workspace data such as clients, projects, leads, quotations, invoices, branding preferences, analytics inputs, team records, and related notes.
  • Files and documents you upload, including contracts, images, PDFs, spreadsheets, and other project assets.
  • Device, diagnostics, and usage information used to maintain app stability, performance, security, and product analytics.
  • Subscription and transaction status information provided through Apple App Store or Google Play purchase systems.
  • Communications you send to DevFlow support or feedback channels.

3. How we collect information

Information may be collected when you:

  • Create an account with email, Google, or Apple sign-in.
  • Enter information into forms inside the app.
  • Upload files to your workspace.
  • Use the app while analytics and crash reporting are enabled.
  • Contact support or request help with your account.

4. Why we use information

We use data to:

  • Provide authentication, sync, storage, and account access.
  • Deliver workspace features such as project tracking, quotations, invoicing, file handling, analytics, and team management.
  • Improve reliability, troubleshoot errors, and understand product performance.
  • Manage subscriptions, entitlements, and support requests.
  • Protect users, prevent abuse, and enforce our terms.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

5. Service providers and processing partners

DevFlow relies on infrastructure and platform providers to operate the service. Based on the current app implementation, these can include:

  • Firebase Authentication for sign-in and account sessions.
  • Cloud Firestore for synced workspace records and app data.
  • Firebase Storage for uploaded files and documents.
  • Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for diagnostics and product performance.
  • Apple and Google services for authentication, app distribution, subscriptions, and purchases where applicable.

We do not sell personal data or share personal data with third-party advertisers for behavioral advertising.

6. Permissions and device access

The app may request access to device capabilities such as camera, photo library, media files, biometric authentication, or notifications when those permissions are relevant to features you choose to use. You can manage permissions in your device settings.

7. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Files, workspace records, and account data may be deleted when you delete content, close your account, or request deletion, subject to legal or operational retention requirements.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect personal data. No service can guarantee absolute security, and users are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure.

9. International data transfers

DevFlow and its service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10. Your rights and choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict, object to, or delete your personal data. DevFlow also includes in-app account deletion and support-assisted requests where needed.

  • To request help with access, export, or deletion, email support@devflow.com.
  • To understand account deletion, visit the Account Deletion page.

11. Children’s privacy

DevFlow is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page and publish the revised version here.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@devflow.com.
