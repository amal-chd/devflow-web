FAQ

Questions before you download.

What does DevFlow actually do?

DevFlow is a mobile workspace for freelancers and small agencies to manage their entire client operation: leads, projects, clients, files, quotations, invoices, analytics, branding, subscriptions, and team activity — in one connected system built for phones.

Does DevFlow handle payment card data directly?

No. Subscriptions are handled entirely through Apple App Store and Google Play purchase frameworks. No card data is stored in the app or on our servers.

How is account deletion handled?

The app includes a full in-app delete-account flow under Security & Privacy. We also have a dedicated Account Deletion page so users can understand their options before downloading.

Who is DevFlow built for?

Solo developers, freelancers, consultants, and small agencies who need one place to run delivery and billing operations from a mobile-first surface.

What languages does DevFlow support?

DevFlow currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese — with more locales coming as the app expands globally.

How does team collaboration work?

Pro plans support up to 3 team members with shared project visibility. Agency plans unlock unlimited collaborators with advanced team coordination.
