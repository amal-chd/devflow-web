FAQ

What people usually need before launch.

What does DevFlow actually do?

DevFlow is a mobile workspace for freelancers and small agencies to manage client operations: projects, leads, clients, files, quotations, invoices, analytics, branding, subscriptions, and team activity.

Does DevFlow collect payment card data directly?

The current codebase handles subscriptions through Apple App Store and Google Play purchase frameworks. Payment processing is handled by those platform providers rather than stored directly in the app.

How is account deletion handled?

The app includes an in-app delete-account flow under Security & Privacy. The website also includes a dedicated account deletion page so users can understand the process before downloading.

Where can users read the legal pages?

This site includes public pages for privacy, terms, support, and account deletion so the links can be used in app store listings, help centers, and onboarding emails.
